# Excel Pivot Analysis Project

## Objective
To analyze an e-commerce dataset using Microsoft Excel pivot tables and create an interactive dashboard with clear business insights.

## Dataset
E-Commerce Orders Dataset containing 10,000 transactions with details such as category, country, customer segment, and sales value.

## Pivot Analysis Overview

### Sales by Country and Customer Segment (Pivot Table)
This pivot table summarizes total sales and transaction counts across countries, enhanced with conditional formatting for quick comparison.

![Sales by Country Pivot](images/pivot_sales_by_country.png)

### Total Sales by Category (Pivot Chart)
A clustered column pivot chart showing the distribution of total sales across product categories.

![Sales by Category Chart](images/pivot_chart_sales_by_category.png)

## Key Insights
1. Electronics is the highest revenue-generating category, contributing the largest share of total sales.
2. The United States leads in total sales, followed by India and the United Kingdom.
3. High-Value customer segment contributes significantly more revenue compared to other segments.
4. Low-Value customers generate higher transaction volumes but lower overall revenue.
5. Sales performance varies across regions, indicating country-specific demand patterns.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Conditional Formatting)
